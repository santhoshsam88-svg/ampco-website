About Ampco

57 years of precision manufacturing excellence serving OEMs across North America.

Our Heritage

Founded in 1967 in Coquitlam, British Columbia, Ampco Manufacturers has grown from a small screen printing shop into a leading supplier of precision graphics and user interface solutions for OEM manufacturers.

Today, we operate from a 63,000 square foot manufacturing facility equipped with state-of-the-art printing, die cutting, and assembly equipment. Our team of over 100 employees brings decades of combined experience to every project.

We're proud to be IATF 16949 certified and to serve as a strategic partner to some of the world's most demanding OEMs in the heavy truck, construction, agriculture, and industrial equipment industries.
