Rubber Keypads

Silicone keypads with precise tactile response, custom durometers, and superior durability.

Our silicone rubber keypads deliver consistent tactile feedback and exceptional durability for demanding user interface applications. From simple single-key designs to complex multi-button assemblies, we engineer keypads that perform reliably across millions of actuations.

Features

Keypad Options

Custom Durometers

Silicone hardness from 30 to 70 Shore A for optimal tactile feel. Multiple durometers in single keypads available.

Backlit Keys

Translucent silicone with integrated light pipes for LED backlighting. Uniform illumination across all keys.

Carbon Pill Contacts

Conductive carbon pills provide reliable electrical contact with PCB pads. Available in various resistances.

Legend Options

Screen printed, laser etched, or epoxy coated legends with excellent abrasion resistance.

Textured Surfaces

Matte, gloss, or textured finishes for enhanced grip and aesthetics.

Over-molding

Multi-color and multi-material keypads with integrated sealing flanges.
