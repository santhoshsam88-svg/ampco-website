Features

Switch Options

Tactile Response

Metal or poly domes provide precise tactile feedback with customizable actuation forces from 150g to 500g. Rated for millions of actuations.

LED Backlighting

Integrated LED or fiber optic backlighting with uniform light distribution. Custom colors and brightness levels available.

Environmental Sealing

IP65 to IP68 rated sealing options protect against dust, water, and chemical intrusion in harsh operating environments.

EMI/RFI Shielding

Integrated shielding layers protect sensitive electronics from electromagnetic interference in industrial settings.

Connector Options

ZIF connectors, pin headers, cable assemblies, or direct PCB integration. Custom tail configurations available.

Display Integration

Cutouts and bezels for LCD, LED, and touchscreen display integration with precise tolerances.
